Giving

"But just as you excel in everything--in faith, in speech, in knowledge, in complete earnestness and in your love for us--see that you also excel in this grace of giving." 2 Cor. 8:7 (NIV)

Throughout Scripture, we are reminded of the importance of giving back a portion of what God has provided for our use. His Word tells us that giving is an act of worship that should be done with a spirit of thanksgiving. Even though we live in a consumer-driven culture that tells us that acquiring "things" is so important, the truth of God's Word hasn't changed - He still calls us to give to others and to the work of His church.

Palm Canyon Community Church offers a number of ways for you to provide financial support to the ministries of our church. See below for several options. Technology has given us new ways of giving, and many of you have expressed an interest in online giving. We are now able to offer you this tool to assist in your giving. Just click on the link below to reach a secure site where you can process your donation.

Online Giving
Website processing of financial gifts provides a fast, convenient, and secure way for you to give one-time gifts by credit card or debit card. Gifts can be designated for Palm Canyon Community Church's General Fund.

Our online giving system allows you to make a contributions one gift at a time. You may use either your PayPal account, credit card or bank account. You may also give a recurring gift by creating a PayPal account and entering mail@palmcanyon.org as the recipient of your donation. Encryption technology is used to ensure that your information is transmitted securely. The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of processing your contribution. Please do not put yourself into debt when giving online. We encourage you to use a debit card and give responsibly.

If you have any questions or concerns about online giving, contact leslie@palmcanyon.org or phone 951.247.2273. Click here for PayPal's Privacy Policy.


Direct Deposit
Electronic checks, or direct deposit, can be arranged to transfer funds from your bank account directly to the church account, monthly or twice-monthly. Contact your bank for information how to set up electronic fund transfers with your general contribution or building fund contribution. Your banker may contact Leslie Hitchcock, our Business Administrator, at 951.247.2273 x21 for check routing information. You will be contacted to confirm the set-up, and you may discontinue the agreement at any time.



Receipts
You will receive a receipt of your tax-deductible gifts in accordance with IRS requirements. Please let us know if you have any specific needs regarding the timing of these notifications, otherwise you will receive only the year-end statements of giving.
