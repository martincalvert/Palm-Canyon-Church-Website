Missions!

Palm Canyon's missions ministry exists to be a partner in the gospel through local, national and worldwide ministries.

International Ministry Oppurtunities

  • Cabo Verde Panama - For $25 you can feed 200 kids for a day. You can donate at the church office or on Sundays in the offering. Designate all donations as "Cabo Verde." If you are interested in praying for, visiting and/or sponsoring this mission work, please contact Jackie Davidson at mail@palmcanyon.org.
  • Rescue African Children - $20 a month provides a child's school fees, books, uniform, daily hot meal and medical needs. rescueafricanchildren.org.
  • Tijuana, Mexico, New Generations Orphanage

Local Ministry Oppurtunities

  • Emma Tautolo [Athletes In Action. Arizona State University] to financially support Emma go to www.cru.org and click "give" and type Emma Tautolo.

  • VBS (Vacation Bible School)
