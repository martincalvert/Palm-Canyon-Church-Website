Adult Ministries

Serving the needs of men and women at Palm Canyon and our community through various events and programs.

Ministries for All Adults

Life Groups

Small group environments where people can share openly, make new friends, find encouragement through the study of God's Word, and "do" life together. The primary "shepherding" arms of Palm Canyon. Life Groups »

Personal Spiritual Trainer (PST)

Leading others in accountable relationships where the Word of God can strengthen and prepare us for greater works.

Care Team

Caring for others (inside and outside of Palm Canyon) during times of crisis and needs. If you are interested in serving in this ministry please email mail@palmcanyon.org

Hospitality Team

The team of people around Palm Canyon on a Sunday making the experience more personal, more interactive, and more friendly through special Sunday themes, between service activities and helping with major events
Ushers: A team that serves and welcomes people throughout the service
Gazebo: An inviting team serving food and beverages to create community
Guest Service: A friendly team helping to inform and answer any questions
Hospitality: Helps create team spirit throughout the year through different events
Undercover Greeters: The most outgoing and friendly people who make others feel welcomed

Intercessory Prayer

An online prayer team interceding in prayer for people and their needs. If you are interested in serving in this ministry please email mail@palmcanyon.org
